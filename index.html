Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about online physiotherapy and our services.

What is online physiotherapy and how does it work?

Online physiotherapy at CoreMotion uses secure video consultations to assess, diagnose, and treat various conditions. Through guided movements, observation, and detailed history-taking, our therapists create personalized treatment plans that you can follow from home with our digital exercise platform.

Is online physiotherapy as effective as in-person sessions?

Yes, research shows that for many conditions, online physiotherapy can be just as effective as in-person care. We use evidence-based protocols and digital tools to ensure you receive high-quality treatment and achieve your recovery goals. For certain complex conditions, we may recommend a hybrid approach.

What conditions can you treat online?

We treat a wide range of conditions including musculoskeletal pain, sports injuries, post-surgical rehabilitation, neurological conditions, women's health issues, and workplace ergonomic problems. During your initial assessment, we'll determine if online care is appropriate for your specific condition.

Do you offer home visits?

We offer limited home visits for patients in and around Whitefield, Bangalore. Please contact us directly to check availability in your area. For most clients, our online services provide comprehensive care without the need for travel.
