Meet Our Founder

"Physiotherapy that teaches, empowers, and transforms the way people move."

I'm Aabha Ingle, the Founder of CoreMotion – The Online Physiotherapist, a platform built from a simple belief: when people understand their body, they heal smarter, move better, and live stronger.

Discover My Journey

A Journey Rooted in Movement

My journey began with a deep curiosity about how movement shapes wellbeing. After completing my professional training in physiotherapy, I pursued advanced study in Musculoskeletal and Rehabilitation Sciences.
This was followed by specialised experience in the UK, where I worked alongside experts in evidence-based, patient-centred physiotherapy. That experience reshaped my entire outlook — physiotherapy, I realised, isn't about pain alone; it's about potential.
The potential to move freely, live confidently, and prevent what most people accept as "normal" pain.

The Challenge That Sparked CoreMotion

Throughout my clinical journey, I began to notice a pattern — not in the body, but in the system around it.

Unnecessary Pain

Too many people live with discomfort that could have been prevented or resolved through timely, guided care.

Myths & Misinformation

The idea that "physiotherapy only works when the therapist touches you" still holds people back from seeking help.

Limited Access

Expert care often feels distant — physically, financially, or emotionally.

These challenges made one thing clear: people weren't lacking motivation — they were lacking clarity, access, and trust. That's how CoreMotion was born — not just as a physiotherapy service, but as a learning space where recovery becomes understanding, not just instructions.

My Vision for CoreMotion

CoreMotion stands for a new kind of physiotherapy — one that merges clinical excellence with education and lifestyle transformation.

Understand Your Body

Education is the foundation of lasting recovery and prevention.

Recover Smarter

Using evidence-based approaches that respect your time and goals.

Live Confidently

Empowering you to take control of your health and movement.

"Here, physiotherapy doesn't end with the last exercise — it continues in the way you move, breathe, and live every day."

Beyond the Clinic: What Drives Me

Physiotherapy is my profession, but human movement is my lifelong passion. Outside clinical practice, I focus on how the mind, body, and habits intersect.

Brain & Lifestyle Science

Understanding how habits, sleep, and mindset accelerate recovery and prevent injuries.

Digital Health Innovation

Bringing physiotherapy home through accessible, tech-supported guidance and remote monitoring.

Holistic Wellbeing

Integrating movement, breathwork, and lifestyle to create sustainable healing and prevention.

My Core Belief

Physiotherapy should not just treat pain — it should teach people how to move, think, and live in harmony with their body.

CoreMotion is my way of making that vision accessible to everyone. It's not just a clinic online — it's a movement for movement.
